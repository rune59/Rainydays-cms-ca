Welcome to an adventure - even on rainy days

Prepare yourself to enjoy rainy days and a beautiful nature in all kinds of weather.

This website presents our company, RainyDays. We are an international company with locations in a number of countries. Our head office is located in Norway.

We sell sportswear in a number of shapes and brands. Both for men and women.

Our typical customers are aged 30-60 years.

We can offer a 3-week exchange right if you are not satisfied with the garment you have bought online from us.

Please take a look at our collection of rain jackets, and feel free to contact us to order jackets.

Our jackets are produced by reputable factories that are known for their quality products. When you buy a garment from us, there is a 5-year guarantee against any damage that may be due to wear and tear or rough use and harsh climate. Our products have been on the market for over 30 years, and we have gained experience that we have continuously used in the further product development.
